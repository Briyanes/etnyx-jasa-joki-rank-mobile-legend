## Content Pillars (4 Pilar)

### 1. HASIL KERJA (Trust) — 40%
Bukti nyata push rank, screenshot before-after, winrate tinggi.

**Format:** Carousel, Single Image, Reels
**Contoh:**
- Before/After rank screenshot (blur ID & nama)
- Winrate report dari match history
- "Customer X naik dari Epic ke Mythic dalam 2 hari"
- Timelapse gameplay push rank

### 2. EDUKASI & TIPS (Value) — 25%
Tips bermain ML yang berguna, meta hero, build guide.

**Format:** Carousel, Reels
**Contoh:**
- "5 Hero Meta Season 35 yang Wajib di-Ban"
- "Build Arlott Tersakit 2026"
- "Cara Rotasi Gold Lane yang Benar"
- "Rank Mythic Honor vs Mythic Glory — Apa Bedanya?"

### 3. PROMO & CTA (Conversion) — 20%
Diskon, rush promo, paket baru, seasonal campaign.

**Format:** Single Image, Story, Reels
**Contoh:**
- "Rush Promo 5 Star Rp 32K — Terbatas!"
- "Promo Ramadan: Diskon 15% semua paket"
- "Paket Gendong Baru — Main Bareng Booster Pro"
- "Grand Opening Skin Giveaway"

### 4. SOCIAL PROOF & INTERAKSI (Engagement) — 15%
Review customer, poll, Q&A, giveaway.

**Format:** Carousel, Story, Reels
**Contoh:**
- Screenshot review customer + bintang 5
- Poll: "Kamu main role apa?"
- Q&A: "Tanya apa aja soal joki ML"
- Giveaway: "Follow + Tag 3 teman = FREE skin"
