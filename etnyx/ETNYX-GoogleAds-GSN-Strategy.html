🔧 Troubleshooting Guide

Issue: Ads Disapproved

Common reasons:
  • ❌ Wording "joki", "push rank", "100% aman"
  • ❌ Landing page mismatch (ads bilang "bantuan", landing bilang "joki")
  • ❌ Misleading claims
Solution: Audit wording. Change ke safe alternatives. Appeal jika necessary.

Issue: Low CTR (<2%)

Possible causes:
  • Ad copy not compelling
  • Headline tidak match search intent
  • Quality Score rendah
Solution: A/B test new headlines. Improve landing page loading. Check QS →improve.

Issue: High CPC (>Rp1.500)

Possible causes:
  • Low Quality Score (<5)
  • Keyword terlalu competitive
  • Bid terlalu tinggi
Solution: Improve QS (better ads + landing page). Lower bids gradual. Pause underperforming keywords.

Issue: No Conversions Reported

Possible causes:
  • GA4 event not firing
  • Conversion tracking not linked
  • User not clicking WhatsApp
Solution: Check GA4 debug mode. Verify conversion action in Google Ads. Test click WA link manually.
