🔍 Competitive Research Findings

Market Analysis - Joki ML Services in Indonesia

What Works in This Market:

  • Speed/Timeline: Players care about HOW FAST they can reach target rank ("1 bulan", "cepat", "tercepat")
  • Safety Assurance: Account security is TOP concern for players ("100% aman", "terenkripsi", "terjaga")
  • Expert Positioning: Players want experts, not just "helpers" ("Expert", "Berpengalaman", "Konsultan")
  • Price Transparency: Showing affordability works ("Mulai Rp 50 Ribu" beats generic "Terjangkau")
  • Free Consultation: Low barrier to entry ("Gratis Hari Ini" outperforms just "Gratis")
  • Social Proof with Rating: "4.8★" is more credible than just "Ratusan Player Puas"
  • Problem Recognition: Starting with their pain point ("Stuck? Ada Solusi Terbaik") = higher CTR
