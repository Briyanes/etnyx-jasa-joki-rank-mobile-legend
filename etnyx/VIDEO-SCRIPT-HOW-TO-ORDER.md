## RECORDING TIPS

### Screen Recording di iPhone:
1. Settings → Control Center → tambah "Screen Recording"
2. Swipe down → tap record → countdown 3 detik
3. Recording mulai (indicator merah di status bar)
4. Selesai → tap status bar merah → Stop

### Screen Recording di Android:
1. Swipe down notification → tap "Screen Recorder"
2. Pilih: Record media sounds (ON), Record microphone (OFF dulu)
3. Mulai recording
4. VO direkam terpisah di CapCut

### Tips Penting:
- **Matikan notifikasi** sebelum recording (Do Not Disturb mode)
- **Charge HP** penuh
- **Hapus tab lain** di browser
- **Gunakan Wi-Fi** biar loading cepat
- **Latihan 1-2x** sebelum recording final
- **Landscape JANGAN** — tetap portrait 9:16
- Rekam **VO terpisah** di tempat sepi, lalu sync di CapCut
- Recording bisa **lebih panjang** dari script — nanti dipotong di CapCut
